## About This Game

You and four friends are enjoying a cruise ride when the boat collides with a rock and sinks.  
You wake up on an unknown island where you meet the other protagonist of this game, with whom you will begin your days of survival. The goal is to find your friends and escape the island.

---

## Key Features

### Dual Protagonists
- Two playable characters (male and female).  
- Story begins with the female protagonist, but you can switch freely anytime.

### The Natives
- Daytime inhabitants who sleep at night.  
- **Interact** with them in various ways:  
  - "Visit" them while they sleep.  
  - Capture and enslave them for labor (logging, excavation, farming).  
  - Control their relationships (allow or restrict lovemaking).  

### Enemies, Monsters & Friends
- Encounter foes and creatures across the island.  
- **Recruit allies** by listening to their stories or taming them (stun instead of kill).  
- Strong allies aid in exploration and combat.  

### Character Customization
- Modify appearances for yourself and NPCs.  
- **Extreme body deformation** (remove limbs!).  
- Dress up characters as desired.  

### Crafting System
- **10+ workbenches** for diverse creations:  
  - Weapons, shields, armor.  
  - Clothes, torture devices, feeding cages.  
  - Sexual toys, seasonal decorations, meals.  

### Survival Activities
- **Farming**: Grow vegetables → cook new dishes.  
- **Fishing**: Catch varied species based on location.  
- **Excavation & Gathering**: Unearth resources.  

### Progression
- Level up via combat, excavation, and gathering.  
- Improved stats (attack power, speed) and new skills.  

### Exploration
- **Caves & Ruins** with escalating difficulty:  
  - Deeper levels = stronger bosses + better loot.  
  - Beware of traps!
